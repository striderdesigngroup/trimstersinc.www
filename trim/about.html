Trimsters, Inc.

A Proud American Builder

Trimsters Inc. has been a part of the residential construction industry since 1997. Each one of our homes is constructed using high quality materials and modern construction techniques.

One man whose story represents the true American dream is Shawn Ogni, President of Trimsters Inc. Over the years Shawn has established a highly successful business and takes the term “family run” to heart. Taking his experience to the next level, he has created a home building company that takes pride in providing homebuyers with a variety of quality options to create their ultimate dream home.

Trimsters Inc. strives to focus on exceptional customer service and to go above and beyond getting the job done. We use our experience and knowledge of homebuilding to create a buyer’s dream house. Analyzing various home plans based on square footage and layout, we offer buyers only the best from which to choose. Our business leaves buyers with the knowledge that their opinions are being heard and valued. Each house is built with thought, time, and dedication.

We stand behind our product and are very happy to welcome you to learn more about what we can do for you.

 

"Trimsters Inc. is proud to be a part of the beautiful neighborhoods of Pecan Grove and Homestead Farm. Close to everything and featuring gracious southern country living. Trimsters Inc. is pleased to offer Craftsman style homes built with high quality materials and tradesman.“
